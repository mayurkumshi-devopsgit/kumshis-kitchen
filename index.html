Our Story

A kitchen full of
sweet memories

Kumshi's Kitchen was born right here in Pimpri Chinchwad — from a deep love of baking and a desire to make every celebration a little more special. What started as cakes for family and friends quickly became a beloved local brand.

Every order is made fresh with the finest ingredients — no preservatives, no compromises. Whether it's a simple birthday cake or an elaborate fondant masterpiece, we pour our heart into every slice.

Proudly serving our local community in Pimpri, Chinchwad, Akurdi, Nigdi, Wakad, Ravet and surrounding areas.
